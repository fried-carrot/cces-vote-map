Canvass feedback — the learning loop

Log real door results and every tract score updates (beta-binomial posterior over the model prior). The calibrated state lives here, compounds with every shift, and is what the raw public data can never contain.

Method

Responsivity = P(Republican-receptive household) × ideological match between the platform index and the tract (Gaussian kernel, σ=25). P(R) is the CCES logistic model post-stratified over race × education × age × income × sex cells built from ACS tables B01001/B03002/B15003/B19001 + 7 more, per tract. Routes: Dijkstra shortest paths on the tract-adjacency graph, greedy marginal yield-per-mile insertion (team orienteering), then 2-opt improvement. Moving the platform slider re-scores every tract and re-optimizes live routes.
